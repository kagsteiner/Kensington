How to play
  • Each player has 15 stones. Red begins.
  • Placement: take turns placing a stone on any empty point.
  • Movement: once all stones are placed, slide one of your stones along a line to an adjacent empty point. If you cannot move, your opponent moves again.
  • Mills: complete a small triangle in your color and you may move one enemy stone anywhere; complete a square and you may move two. Never more than two per turn.
  • Win by occupying all six corners of a white hexagon or a hexagon of your own color.
